Our Services

Bookkeeping and Accounting

We provide bookkeeping and accounting services to small to medium enterprises. We ensure that our clients get accurate and timely financial reports in a format that they can easily understand. Our job is to simplify the numbers in a manor that our clients can easily understand. Your finances are the life blood of your business and we ensure that you always have your hand on the pulse of your business.

Consulting and Advisory

Our consulting and advisory unit will sit down with you to understand the specific and unique requirements of your business. We will then design a business strategy that addresses the specifics of your business and that will ensure your business gets to the next level.
This may include an operations strategy, a marketing plan or process restructuring amongst other solutions.

Municipal Billing Queries

Many clients experience wrong billings from council and these wrong billings usually affect the profits of the business because they are usually overstated. Our team will analyse your municipal account statements over a period and we can tell whether there is reasonable cause to raise a query with the council and get the account resolved. We will them lodge a query with council and follow it up to its conclusion so that you may get a refund where necessary.
